Tampico/Madero Chamber of Commerce

This is my home town in Mexico and it's a costal city in the Mexican Golf with lots of tourism.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Tampico/Madero Chamber of Commerce is to introduce and share its beutiful places like the beach, the parks, downtown area, cathedrals, businesses, and temple. This will help every tourist interested in visiting Tampico/Madero and the people in Tampico and surrounding cities to participate in the social events happening.

Scenarios

1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
3. What has been the population growth in the area?
